V4V Open Lessons 

We host monthly interactive Value 4 Value lessons on HiveTalk, driven by community contributions and open to all who want to share or enhance their skills. In the rapidly evolving Bitcoin space, staying informed is best through community-driven content. Participants can share their knowledge, including their lightning address or Nostr pubkey for direct donations from learners. Explore our searchable repository of lessons for valuable insights. Reach out if you'd like to contribute your own lessons.


Decentralised Autonomous Education System (DAES)

The DAES learning community provides a platform for aspiring learners to submit their Bitcoin project ideas for potential funding and mentorship. By leveraging the decentralized nature of DAES, we create an inclusive environment where innovative ideas can thrive. We also manage and moderate the Stacker News /~Education territory, where learners can submit their projects for consideration. Additionally, we offer a Signal chat for collaboration and mentorship, enhancing our support for promising proposals. Anyone interested in supporting these projects can contribute to our learner fund, helping to bring innovative ideas to fruition.









PlanB Network

The School of Bitcoin is proud to be a part of the Plan B Network, an initiative that perfectly aligns with our mission to promote open-source education and community building. As part of this collaboration, we teach using the Plan B Network's curriculum, which is designed to provide comprehensive, high-quality Bitcoin education. Through this partnership, we contribute to a global movement dedicated to empowering local Bitcoin communities with the knowledge and tools they need to thrive. By utilising the Plan B Network’s resources, tutorials, and events, we are helping to build a more informed and connected global Bitcoin ecosystem.
