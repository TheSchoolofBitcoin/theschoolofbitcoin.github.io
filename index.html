V4V Open Lessons 

Every week, we facilitate interactive Value 4 Value lessons that are fueled by the collective efforts of our community and accessible to all individuals interested in sharing and honing their skills. Given the rapid pace of developments in the realm of Bitcoin, the most effective approach to staying well-informed is by fostering an environment of dynamic content creation that is driven by the community. In this collaborative framework, any willing participant can impart their knowledge during these lessons, and they are encouraged to include their unique lightning address within the video and repository, enabling learners to contribute donations directly to the instructors.


Discover a vast collection of lessons conveniently stored in a searchable repository. Gain access to a wide range of knowledge and expertise. If you're interested in sharing your own lessons, don't hesitate to reach out to us. 


Decentralised Autonomous Education System (DAES)⚡

The DAES is a learning community that empowers aspiring learners with a platform to submit their Bitcoin project ideas for potential funding and mentorship opportunities. Our team of experts covers a diverse range of domains, including Bitcoin AI, NOSTR, and lightning, ensuring comprehensive guidance and support for all submitted proposals. By leveraging the decentralised nature of DAES, we aim to foster an inclusive environment where innovative ideas can flourish and receive the necessary resources to transform into successful Bitcoin projects.


Contact us with your project ideas.
